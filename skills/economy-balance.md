# Economy Balance Skill

## Purpose

Use this guide when adjusting economy or progression values in **Yelkenli Yaşam Tycoon**.

## Balance Philosophy

- Money, followers, and XP should work together as connected progression layers.
- Route rewards should feel important because routes are a core pillar of the game loop.
- Content rewards should be useful for growth and momentum, but content should not become spammable or dominate all other play.
- Sponsor thresholds should create achievable milestones that feel motivating, especially in the early game.
- Upgrade costs should create meaningful decisions and medium-term planning.
- Daily bonuses should feel rewarding without breaking pacing or economy balance.

## Early-Game Expectations

- Early game should feel active and rewarding but not broken.
- The player should not go bankrupt too easily early on.
- The player should have enough room to recover from small mistakes.
- Progress should feel steady enough to maintain motivation.

## How To Evaluate Economy Changes

- Check how a value change affects money flow, follower growth, XP gain, and upgrade timing.
- Check whether route rewards still matter after the change.
- Check whether content remains useful without becoming the optimal spam strategy.
- Check whether sponsor thresholds feel reachable at the intended pace.
- Check whether daily bonuses are supportive rather than economy-breaking.

## Change Rules

- Explain economy changes before implementation.
- Avoid random value changes without design reasoning.
- Do not tune numbers only by instinct; connect them to pacing, motivation, and risk/reward.
- Prefer small balance adjustments over large swings.
- Preserve existing working progression unless there is a clear balance problem to solve.

## Practical Reminder

- When changing economy values, test the player experience in the early game first.
- Keep balance changes understandable enough that future agents can maintain them safely.
