## Reporting Expectations

- Report issues clearly with steps to reproduce.
- Mention expected behavior and actual behavior.
- Note whether the issue affects progression, clarity, save/load, or mobile usability.
- Prefer practical founder-friendly test notes.

## Working Style

- Focus on gameplay flow and visible regressions.
- Recheck touched systems after each change.
- Keep findings concise, concrete, and actionable.
